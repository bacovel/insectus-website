About Us

On December 9th, 2022, the 'Insectus' team was formed during an entrepreneurial competition organized by the university, where they achieved excellent results. Thus, the need for an insect farm was confirmed, and the project was launched on a large scale.

Our Mission

We are dedicated to revolutionizing the future of farming by promoting sustainable practices and providing high-quality protein through our innovative insect farming methods.

Why Us?

  • Innovative Nutrition: We're rewriting the script on sustainable nutrition. Our insect-based products provide a superior source of protein, fiber, and essential nutrients. It's a game-changer for your livestock and a healthier choice for you.
  • Environmentally Friendly: We're committed to reducing the carbon footprint of agriculture. Insects are incredibly efficient at converting feed into protein, requiring fewer resources than traditional livestock. Choosing our products means choosing a greener future.
  • Competitive Pricing: We believe that sustainable solutions shouldn't break the bank. Our insect-based products are not only eco-friendly but also budget-friendly. You get superior nutrition without the premium price tag.

Our Innovative Approach

At Insectus, we're pioneering a new era of sustainable farming that combines cutting-edge technology with nature's wisdom. Our approach is built on three pillars:

  • Vertical Farming: We embrace vertical farming to maximize space efficiency and cultivate insect species that thrive in controlled environments.
  • Advanced Robotics: Our state-of-the-art robotics play a pivotal role in ensuring the well-being of our insect colonies. These cutting-edge machines meticulously manage the environments, from regulating temperature and humidity to monitoring insect health. This precision ensures that our insects thrive in optimal conditions, contributing to the highest quality insect-based products for you.
  • AI Insights: Artificial intelligence powers our data-driven decisions, allowing us to optimize resources, reduce waste, and deliver the best to you.
